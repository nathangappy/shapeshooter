Shapeshooter

Destroy as many circles as you can before you run out of health

    Instructions

  • 1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move your shooter left & right
  • 2. Use the spacebar to destroy targets by shooting space missiles from your shooter
  • 3. Your health is damaged at the end of every level. The more targets on the screen at the end of each level, the more damage you take.
  • 4. You accumulate score points for each target you take out.
